That time when we climbed a mountain and almost killed by a bear.

During the summer I received a called with one of my friends and he tells me about the trip to Maine and Mount Washington. Me being a thrill seeker I was already on it. Boy little did I know about the things that would happen on the trip.

Day one: We reached Maine, had some nice lobsters and rested for tommorow for our trek to Mount Washington.

Day two: The day was warm about 25 degrees Celsius (85 degree F). We were all prepped to climb. In for 30 minutes a guy from our group, who never climbed. Looking at him I thought the only thing he has climbs were the stairs. He asks how long it? "about 5 hours" another dude said. That face of horror was hilarious, I shall never forget. 15 minutes Later, he quit and went back. About 4 hours into the climb. I was exhausted I could not feel my legs. I was leading in the group so I rested for 5 minutes. This is where everything gets dark (well brown). I start to see a brown furry thing about 100 ft away. I could see it well. A guy comes behind I point at this furry thing and ask him if can see what is it? His faced turned as he saw something like a bear(it was a bear). Now I am exhausted to the point I could not climb anymore. My heart sank. I started to sweat and screaming "runnnnnn".

I have never sprinted the way as I did. I ran as my life depended on it. the road became really tough and we took the hardest trek for the mountain (Cause why not? Stupid me). 30 minutes later I reached at the top. Lying there dead almost passed out. My group came quite later. So much happened I forgot the time to see the view quite later. When I finally got some energy I stood up and saw the view. This had to one of the best views I have seen. It was one of best days of my life.

Would I do it all over again? Absolutely. Because. Only those who dare truly live.

PS: Do not try this. No seriously.
